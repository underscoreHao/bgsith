About Us

 

Bulgarian Sith Order
Information
The Bulgarian Sith Order is a voluntary organization of Star Wars fans, cloaked by the Dark Side of the Force.

It is an independent structure, designed for imposing the Sith ideals - maintaining peace, order, progress, might and power.

History
The Bulgarian Sith Order was officially established on 28 April 2006 by Dark Lord of the Sith (Georgi Popov) as a fraction of the Bulgarian Star Wars Federation. He gets considerate help from some Federation members to accomplish his idea of a completely Sith Site and Order where Sith traditions will be taught.

Nowadays The Bulgarian Sith Order is headed by both Dark Lord of the Sith and Darth Amidala and a Sith Council, consisting of 6 members. This is our website and you are welcome also in our discussion board here - where you can find an International zone.

Goals
Our goal is to popularize Sith and Sith traditions, to reach full power in the Force and of course - Galaxy domination :)

Our activities
We are trying to locate and train powerful force-users in the ways of the Sith.

We discuss and spread the Sith traditions to those who are worthy.

We regularly organize various events like competitions and Sith meetings.
